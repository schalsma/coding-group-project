Frequently Asked Questions

How do I see my calendar?

Click on the Today's Schedule box on the home page or click on the Schedule button at the bottom of the screen on any page.

How do I see only my school assignments?

Click on the To Do list box on the home page or click on the Assignments button at the bottom of the screen on any page.

Where should I go for more help with homework?

Try visiting the Resources page. The button is located at the bottom of every page.

How do I add something to my calendar?

Click "Create Event" on the Schedule page and fill in your event's information.

How do I add an assignment to my list?

Your assignments are pulled directly from your school's learning management system, so please contact your teacher to have them add it into the system.

How do I reprioritize my assignments?

You can click on each assignment on the Assignments page and drag it to the order you would like. You can also add a difficuly level using the dropdown menu to help you remember what kinds of assignments you have ahead of you.
